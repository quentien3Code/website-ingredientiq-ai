Frequently Asked Questions

How are flags generated?

Our AI cross-references scanned ingredients with authoritative databases (FDA, USDA, EFSA, PubMed) to identify allergens, processing levels, and health risks. Each flag is backed by cited scientific evidence and regulatory guidelines.

Do you store my scans or personal data?

No. We use minimal data collection with encrypted scans that aren't stored permanently. You own your data, and we never sell it to third parties. HIPAA-aware data handling practices ensure your privacy.

Will you recommend alternative products?

No. We educate, we don't sell. IngredientIQ flags risks and explains ingredients‚Äîwe never recommend replacement products or earn affiliate commissions. Your decisions stay yours.

What devices/platforms are supported?

IngredientIQ will launch on both iOS and Android in Q1 2026. Early beta access starts Q4 2025 for founding members.

How is per-container math shown?

We calculate total amounts per serving and per entire container, showing you exactly how much sodium, sugar, or other nutrients you're really consuming‚Äînot just the misleading "per serving" numbers.

When does it launch?

Private beta begins Q4 2025 for early access members. Public launch is scheduled for Q1 2026. Join the waitlist to be notified when your spot opens.

Is there a cost?

Early access is free for the first 1,000 users with lifetime access. After that, subscription plans will be announced.
